Lantern Sage

Manuscript ritual with carved accents.

This direction is not about layout. It is only about how the product should look at the level of letterform and line treatment. The title should feel copied by hand, half-preserved, slightly ceremonial. The lines should feel scored, broken, and symbolic rather than neat and digital.
